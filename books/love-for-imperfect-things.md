# Love For Imperfect Things by Haemin Sunim

## Page 1-20

- Do not be a doorstep by being too good

- Living life according to other's desires, we neglect our own
- Do not suppress your feelings or emotions. Remember like still water that can be contaminated or fetid
- Learn to express one's feelings without agonizing over it
- Learn to say no. If a relationship gets stained because you said no, then it was not a good relationship to begin with
- Learn to look at your own flaws with love. Look to grow with your flaws and empathize with others
- Learn to realize that there is no such thing as a perfect human being. Do not be afraid to fail and take risks
- Learn to not judge people because they may have secrets or things that they do not say to others
- Learn to not compare your own to others since we do not know what is happening on the inside
- Learn to be confident about yourself regardless of your unattractive qualities
- Learn to compare yourself with your previous self and be determined to provide an effort
- Learn to not let criticism hinder you from your goals
